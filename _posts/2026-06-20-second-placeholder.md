This exists so the homepage list has more than one entry and you can see the
reverse-chronological ordering.

Posts are just Markdown files in `_posts/` named `YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md`. The
date in the filename controls the ordering; the `description` in the front
matter is the one-liner shown on the homepage.
